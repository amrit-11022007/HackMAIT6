Our Vision: Transparent Governance

Empowering every citizen to drive positive change in their local community.

Our Mission

To empower every citizen to easily report civic issues like sewage, road damage, or waste management, and ensure timely resolution through accountability, technology, and community participation.

How It Works

  • Report Problem: Citizens upload issue details with photo, location, and description
  • Verify & Forward: The system automatically routes the complaint to the concerned department.
  • Track Progress: Users can monitor the live status and updates of their complaint.
  • Resolved & Reviewed: Once fixed, citizens receive confirmation and can rate the service.
